Clean Offer

As a service to our clients, we subscribe to CleanOffer. CleanOffer is an online tool that provides the consumer with complete, real-time listing data. Not available to the general public, access to CleanOffer’s listings is limited to clients who have been granted login rights by their Agent. Set your own search criteria to receive email alerts for new properties. Track your “Favorite” properties and receive alerts when there is a change in price or status. This exclusivity gives the serious Homebuyer a decided edge in today’s fast market. Click on the CleanOffer logo below to sign up with us.
