Rita V. Schmid

Rita V. Schmid is a licensed California broker with exceptional business acumen in evaluating and forecasting market trends. She comes from a family heavily invested in rental homes, industrial buildings and a lakeside resort in Northern California. She provides clients with an in-depth knowledge of the real estate market and is experienced in adapting to its complex demands. Her exceptional work ethic, combined with excellent verbal and written communication skills, gives her clients professional service, highly informed real estate advice and negotiation skills that ensure protection of their real estate equity.

Rita holds a B.S. in human resources organizational behavior from the University of San Francisco, and brings to real estate over 25 years experience within the sales and marketing field. She has also been licensed in New York City and has managed client relationships and direct marketing campaigns for large companies.

Rita’s professional objectives are to inspire, motivate and encourage clients to be successful in the sale or purchase of their property. Rita’s approach in selling a property is to guide her clients through the marketing process, help them prepare their property to show and negotiate offers. Buying property is a serious investment, so Rita will provide her clients with in-depth information on neighborhoods, deliver a comprehensive comparative market analysis, negotiate offers, arrange for financing if required and manage the transaction.

Roxana Melgarejo

Roxana Melgarejo has been in the real estate and mortgage markets for almost a decade, working for buyers and sellers from all over the Bay Area. Her interest in real estate began back in 1999, when she purchased her first condominium in the Bay Area. Much intrigued by the market and greatly admiring her realtor, she has dedicated herself to providing her clients with the best service and resources.

Roxana’s knowledge and experience in Single Family Homes, Condos, Lofts and New Developments have helped her negotiate successfully many transactions for her clients in the Bay Area. She prides herself on a caring professionalism that makes complex transactions as smooth as possible for all her clients, from the first-time homebuyer to the seasoned real estate investor.

Roxana was born in Lima-Peru, and at fifteen, she moved to California with her family. After graduating high school and attending college in Hayward California, she chose to travel the world. She began a career as a Flight Attendant with one of the top four U.S. Carriers: Northwest Airlines. Based at different hubs – Michigan, Minneapolis, New York City, and beautiful San Francisco – allowed her to learn more about America and visit many countries in Europe, Asia, and Central and South America.

With her solid foundation in customer service, honed from fourteen years of experience as a flight attendant, Roxana has great communication and people skills. Bilingual in Spanish and English, she can negotiate successfully the hot retirement/resort properties in Mexico and South America as well as the intricacies of the Bay Area housing market.

She is an active member of the San Francisco Association of Realtors and the National and California Association of Realtors. Contact the RnR Team for a complementary consultation, from homes, condominiums to commercial and investment properties.
